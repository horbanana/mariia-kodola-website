About Me

Every love story deserves to be told beautifully

Hello, I'm Mariia — a wedding photographer with a passion for genuine emotion, soft light, and timeless imagery. I believe the best photographs happen when couples feel completely at ease, so I bring warmth, calm energy, and an unobtrusive presence to every wedding day.

My style is cinematic and editorial — I'm drawn to quiet in-between moments, the way light falls across a veil, and the subtle glances that tell the whole story. Each gallery I deliver is carefully curated and edited to feel cohesive, elegant, and entirely yours.
